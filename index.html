About Me

I'm a full stack software engineer, graduate of UCLA, and Hack Reactor/ MakerSquare's 700+ hour Advanced Software Engineering Immersive. My love for the software engineering world came somewhat surprisingly and with the help of a great friend. He, also being CS grad from MIT introduced me to new possibilities within the exciting and fast paced world of software. From the moment I created my first rock, paper, scissors game I knew where I ultimately belonged.

Fast forward to now, I've led and collaborated with engineers on multiple applications shifting between server-side and front-end roles. I love working with UI, UX and frameworks including Angular.JS & React.JS and also have substantial experience with back-end technologies including Node.JS, Express, MySQL, PostgreSQL and Bookshelf.JS. Insatiably curious, I love a fresh challenge, picking up new technologies and using my creativity to develop meaningful applications with valuable outcomes.

When I'm not diving into code, I love to surf, snowboard and maintain a healthy lifestyle. I have also been crazy about cars since just about the day I was born and in the near future plan on racing semi-competitively. Oh, and just as a fair warning I might claim the title of ping pong champion if we ever happen to share the same office.
